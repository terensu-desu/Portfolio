Life is exciting, just as other cultures are. Although I am American, I was born in Spain, and that is where my interest in foreign cultures began. Utilizing modern technologies

Jumping into web development and programming, I've focused on studying and practicing popular, modern toolsets. Bootstrap, Material, jQuery, React, and Angular are some of the primary toolsets that I've worked with. I also have practical experience with Git and GitHub.

A user turned developer

As a user, I often find myself considering the design choices of other developers. Sometimes the execution of an idea is good but lacking in some way for the user. Having experiences like these reminds me to switch perspectives while designing an application. Happy users can generate a lot of conversation.
